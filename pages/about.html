About us

Picco Cabs is an emerging indian startup based on Madurai to provide Hassle free & Top class cabs services at your doorsteps in lowest prices. We are thrilled to be a part of leading travel companies in South India. While we remain the B2B operator for various popular aggregators.We are now available for direct booking to ensure the customers gets cabs for best prices. We provide One way drop, Two way round trips and Multi city drop and round trips along with Local and Rental packages. We work round the clock to provide 24*7 Services at your doorsteps. We have a strong fleet base starting from Mini(Hatchback), Sedan, SUV, Innova, Tempo. We provide every kind of Fleets according to the need of our clients. All our Vehicles are very well maintained & Well sanitized to provide safe travel experience for our guests. We always ensure to be well on time and all our drivers are well trained to drive professionally & well behaved to ensure our guest enjoy the best of the travel experience. We are also proud to be the first one to introduce Share Taxi / Cab pool services in our major routes. Over the course of 8 years, we have set the standard of quality and service that the rest of our industry strives to meet. Picco cabs also offer special tour packages which comprises the package of travel, food, accommodation, sightseeing and activities. We customize the tours based on Group trips, Family tour, Honeymoon tours, Adventurous tours & Nature tours with Eco retreat. We cover all the major cities in South india connecting Chennai , Madurai , Coimbatore , Tirunelveli, Pondicherry , Trivandrum , Dindigul , Salem , Bangalore , Trichy , Erode , Tanjore , Rameshwaram , Kanyakumari , Kodaikanal , Munnar , Tirupathi & Ooty.
